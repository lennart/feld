FELD_ Help

Welcome to the interactive virtual sound installation! You will need:

  • A smartphone
  • A browser
    Chrome/Android 5+ or Safari/iOS 14+
  • Headphones
  • An additional phone battery or power bank
    We recommend at least 2200 mAh, plus a cable to connect to your phone
  • Mobile internet
    approx. 300 MB will be required, book e.g. a data flat rate for the day, if necessary

Things to prepare beforehand:

  1. Charge your phone and power bank

  2. Pack your headphones, power bank and charging cable

  3. Come to Tempelhofer Feld, Berlin

We also recommend using a phone case with a strap.

Preparations at the site:

  1. Switch on your mobile internet

  2. Switch on Allow location detection

  3. Put on your headphones

Great, now you’re ready to go! Just one last piece of advice: Don’t lock your screen. If you do lock your screen, you’ll have to reload the page.

…and run, bike or walk around.

When you’ve finished, just close the website.

NOTE: Be aware of your surroundings: There are other people and objects all around you. When you are wearing headphones and concentrating on what you are listening to your reaction time may be slower.
